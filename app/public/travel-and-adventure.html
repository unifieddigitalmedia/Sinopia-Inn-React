PLAN A TRIP

Jamaica has long been a jewel in the Caribbean and famously known for its legendary rum and reggae, its laid back and relaxed atmosphere, and its dynamic people. Portland, Jamaica has a lot to offer - but there’s far more to discover. It is one of the few destinations in the world where beaches have remained completely unspoiled. It is a destination that unlocks the mystery of nature – an oasis with amazing sunsets and oceanic views which are an ideal escape for island lovers. Trips to other parishes are available but a trip to a local beach is a must. Portland is famous for its Winnifred, San San and Frenchman’s Cove beaches. A day trek to the Blue Mountains will blow you away with its stunning views and sparkling Falls. And then there is the Blue Lagoon adding that WOW factor with its deep blue waters that change colour, during the day, through every shade of jade and emerald.
